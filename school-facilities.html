School Facilities

Hostel

+

-

Our hostel provides a safe and comfortable living environment for students. With well-furnished rooms, 24/7 security, and dedicated staff, we ensure that our students feel at home while pursuing their studies.

We offer nutritious meals, recreational facilities, and a supportive community to foster personal growth and academic success.

Our hostel is designed to be a home away from home, where students can thrive academically and personally.

Classroom

+

-

Our classrooms are designed to provide a conducive learning environment for students. Each classroom is equipped with modern teaching aids, including smart boards and projectors, to enhance the learning experience.

We prioritize student engagement and interaction, encouraging collaborative learning through group activities and discussions.

The spacious layout of our classrooms allows for flexible seating arrangements, accommodating various teaching styles and learning preferences.

Library

+

-

Our library is a hub of knowledge, offering a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources. With comfortable reading areas and study rooms, students can immerse themselves in their studies.

We also provide access to online databases and research materials, ensuring that our students have the tools they need to succeed academically.

Our dedicated staff is always available to assist students in finding the resources they need and to promote a love of reading and learning.

Computer Lab

+

-

Our computer lab is equipped with the latest technology, providing students with access to modern computers and software. We offer courses in computer literacy, programming, and digital skills to prepare students for the future.

The lab is supervised by experienced instructors who provide guidance and support to help students develop their technical skills.

We also encourage collaborative learning and project-based activities to enhance students' problem-solving abilities and creativity.

Transportation

+

-

B.B Memorial English Secondary School provides reliable and safe transportation for all students. Our fleet includes two full-size school buses and one mini vehicle for off-road and remote routes.

One long bus travels from Rakshe to Nepaltar, dropping students, then continues to Simle to pick up additional students. The second bus starts from Pamujwa, drops students at Nepaltar, and then goes to Santidada to receive more students. Our mini vehicle is dedicated to carrying students from off-road and Ranke areas, ensuring every child has access to school.

All vehicles operate full-time and are driven by experienced, professional drivers who prioritize student safety and comfort. Our transportation service covers all major routes, making daily commutes easy and secure for our students.

School Area

+

-

B.B Memorial English Secondary School is situated in a serene and picturesque location, surrounded by lush greenery and beautiful landscapes. The school campus spans a large area, providing ample space for academic buildings, sports facilities, and recreational areas.

Our classrooms are spacious and well-ventilated, equipped with modern teaching aids to enhance the learning experience. The school also features dedicated areas for extracurricular activities, including sports fields, a playground, and a multipurpose hall.

The peaceful environment of our school area fosters a conducive atmosphere for learning and personal growth, allowing students to thrive both academically and socially.

Extra Curricular Activities

+

-

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities to complement our academic programs. Students can participate in sports, arts, music, drama, and various clubs that cater to diverse interests.

Our extracurricular programs are designed to foster teamwork, leadership skills, and personal development. We encourage students to explore their passions and talents outside the classroom.

Through these activities, we aim to create a well-rounded educational experience that promotes holistic growth and lifelong learning.

Sports Facilities

+

-

Our school boasts excellent sports facilities, including a well-maintained playground, basketball and volleyball courts, and open fields for various outdoor activities. We encourage students to participate in sports to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

We organize regular sports events and competitions to foster a spirit of healthy competition and camaraderie among students. Our dedicated sports coaches provide training and support to help students excel in their chosen sports.

Through our sports programs, we aim to instill lifelong values of discipline, perseverance, and a healthy lifestyle in our students.
