Message from the Principal

Dear Students, Parents, and Guardians,

Welcome to B.B Memorial English Secondary School. It is my great pleasure to extend a warm greeting to all of you as we embark on another exciting academic year. At B.B M.E.S, we are dedicated to fostering a nurturing and stimulating environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Our dedicated team of educators is passionate about inspiring a love for learning and empowering students to reach their fullest potential.

We believe that education is not just about imparting knowledge, but about shaping character, building confidence, and preparing our students to face the challenges of an ever-changing world. Our curriculum is designed to encourage critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration, while our co-curricular and extra-curricular activities provide opportunities for students to discover and develop their unique talents and interests.

At B.B M.E.S, we value the importance of strong partnerships between home and school. We encourage open communication and active involvement from parents and guardians, as we believe that working together creates a supportive community that helps our students excel and become responsible, compassionate citizens. Together, we can instill in our children the values of respect, integrity, and perseverance.

We are proud of our school's achievements and the positive impact our students have made in various fields. Our alumni have gone on to excel in higher education and professional careers, and we continue to support and guide our current students on their journey toward success. We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation, ensuring that our teaching methods and facilities remain up-to-date and effective.

Thank you for being a part of the B.B M.E.S family. I look forward to working together to make this year a successful and memorable one for our students. Let us continue to inspire, support, and celebrate each other as we strive for excellence in all that we do.

Sincerely,

Mrs. Tika Basnet
